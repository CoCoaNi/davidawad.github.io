My name is David Awad,


I'm an aspiring philosopher, web developer, designer, programmer, physicist, mathematician, engineer, poet and person.
I spend all of my free time building things, and trying to improve myself for the people around me.

If you had asked me where I thought I would be five years ago I can guarantee you I wouldn't have said a software developer at an amazing company. I am so thankful for all the opportunities I've been given this past year. Everything just seems to be going my way.

My dreams have certainly varied as of late, hoping that I can accomplish so many things with my career. Necessarily because my career is philosophically the most important thing to me, but because there is so much I haven't done that some of my more talented peers have.

I feel like I have so much more to still do with my life that other people have, even some of my friends in my year have had interviews for my dream job at google.

All I know is that I want to do and create a lot more before I graduate college and not be a GPA slapped into a database.
